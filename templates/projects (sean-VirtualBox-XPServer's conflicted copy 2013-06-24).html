Projects

Over time I have worked on many different projects; here, I list a smaller subset.
This list will grow as I have more projects and more time to work on the site!


Oscilloscope

My sophmore year in college I built a fully functioning 5 MHz oscilloscope, it runs on an Altera FPGA, it was designed, programed, soldered, wire wrapped, and documented by myself, if you want to read through the documentation and code you can find it here.



Touch Keypad

As a part of the Oscilloscope project my roommate and I decided to work together to design, self-print, and assemble our own capacitive touch keypads in order to operate our oscilloscope. More information is in the Oscilloscope Manual.



Science Olympiad

Throughout high school I participated in a variety of events in Science Olympiad. Medaling nationally in Electric Vehicle, Trajectory, Mission Possible, It's about Time, and Physics Lab. To this day I continue to help run competitions in Southern California.
